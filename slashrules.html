How to “Slash it”?

  • 1 Go to “Slash It” page, select desired product, and enter its detail page;
  • 2 Click the “Slash it” button, you will immediately slash a price! And a Sharing Box will be popped out.
  • 3 Choose one social media and share the product with your friends, invite them to help you slash the price;
  • 4 Buy the item at the slashed price.

Rules

  • 1 How long does a slashing activity last?

    • Each slashing activity lasts 72 hours. If the remaining time of an item’s activity is less than 72 hours, the remaining time is valid.

  • 2 How can I slash a product to the target price?

    • In the specified time, you need to invite enough friends to help you achieve the target price.

  • 3 If I didn’t slash a product to its target price, can I buy the product at slashed price?

    • Yes! Within the specified time, you can purchase the item at the current slashed price.

  • 4 Can I “Slash” the same product twice or more?

    • Sorry, each user only has one opportunity to slash per item (sku), no matter the slashing activity succeeded or failed. But don’t worry, you can choose other products for slashing.

  • 5 For each day, how many slash opportunities does each user have?

    • Each user only has three slash opportunities every day.

  • 6 Under what condition will a slashing activity fail?

    • 1. Fail to place “Slashing Orders “in the required time;
    2. Fail to pay for “Slashing Orders” in the required time.

Tips: Users who help slashing price need to log in Tomtop at first!
